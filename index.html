Professional Summary

Dan is a senior cybersecurity, SIEM, cloud, and AI/ML engineering professional with deep experience across Splunk Cloud, detection engineering, security operations, enterprise telemetry, data quality, cloud security, and automation.

He brings hands-on experience with modern security and cloud technologies, including Splunk Cloud, Google SecOps, Wiz, AWS, Google Cloud, Azure, Cloudflare, Git, GitHub Desktop, web deployment workflows, and CI/CD-style publishing pipelines.

His background spans ingestion architecture, CIM normalization, datamodel engineering, search optimization, queue health, endpoint telemetry integrity, dashboards, SOC enablement, MDR support, compliance visibility, malware remediation, patch validation, and enterprise Windows automation.

Dan also brings personnel management and technical leadership experience, including team guidance, employee development, hiring participation, performance evaluation, stakeholder coordination, and translating complex engineering issues into clear operational direction.

Alongside his cybersecurity background, Dan has independently developed roughly 100 AI/ML projects, including local LLM deployments, RAG systems, transcription and diarization tools, automation workflows, knowledge engineering, and AI-assisted operational analysis.
